About

Born and raised in glorious Michigan City, IN, I attended Indiana University where I double-majored in Telecommunications and Germanic Studies, and minored in Marketing. As a participant of the 30th Congress-Bundestag Youth Exchange Program, I lived in Germany for a year where I studied Marketing for a semester at the Fachhochschule Mainz and worked as a design intern at PRIME Research in Mainz.

Music, emerging technologies, design, travel, and video games are some things in which I am interested.

Currently, I'm designing web features, graphics, and components for the Tire Rack.
